Spider-man

About Spider-man 🕷️

Spider-Man is a superhero in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, he first appeared in the anthology comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 in the Silver Age of Comic Books. Considered one of the most popular and commercially successful superheroes, he has been featured in comic books, television shows, films, video games, novels, and plays.

Actors who have played Spider-Man 🕷️

  • Tom Holland

All Movies Starring These Actors: A Comprehensive List 🕷️

Beginning in the 2000s, Columbia developed a film trilogy starring Tobey Maguire as Spider-Man: Spider-Man (2002), Spider-Man 2 (2004), and Spider-Man 3 (2007). The original film in particular greatly influenced Hollywood conception of superheroes. A reboot series featuring Andrew Garfield as the titular hero followed in the 2010s, beginning with The Amazing Spider-Man (2012). Sony then entered a partnership with Marvel Studios under a renewed licensing agreement, yielding a succession of films that incorporated the character into the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The MCU portrays an incarnation of Spider-Man played by Tom Holland.
